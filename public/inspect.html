Image Inspector — CommerceTools color check

Drop one or more images and get the color model (sRGB / CMYK / uncalibrated), whether an ICC profile is embedded, and whether the image will pass or fail in CommerceTools. This diagnoses the "Unsupported image data. Not able to identify the color model" error seen in the pipeline log. Nothing is saved or modified: read-only.

🔍

Drop images here

or click to select — JPG, PNG, TIFF, WebP, AVIF

OK — sRGB with ICC profile. CommerceTools accepts it.
WARN — RGB with no embedded profile (uncalibrated). May be rejected.
FAIL — CMYK or unrecognized color model. CommerceTools rejects it for sure.
The fix for WARN/FAIL is to reprocess the image through the Optimizer, which embeds a standard sRGB profile.
